The Dinners

Ho Ho Hosts! is a MERN stack RESTful application with authentication. Users are able to host a Christmas dinner with space at the table or search for a spare place, aimed at young professionals away from home. Individually and pair programmed, with scalability potential to give people a means to engage with others. I built the full RESTful route for the dinners resource, backend routes set-up, Google API, and comments function.
